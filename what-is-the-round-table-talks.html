The Round Table Talks

A private service for people who mean business. We bring the right people together around live opportunities — introductions across sectors, acquisitions, and project delivery — from the first conversation to post-completion.

Bring Your Opportunity

The Approach

This Is Not Networking. This Is How Opportunities Get Traction.

The Round Table Talks exists for one reason: to help live opportunities move forward. Whether you need the right introduction to a sector you don't yet have access to, the right team around a site you're looking to acquire, or the right expertise on a project already in motion — this is where that happens.

We work with a maximum of eight people at the table. Not because it's a format — because it's the number at which everyone in the room is accountable, every conversation is direct, and every outcome is possible.

You come with a live opportunity. We bring the right people. What happens next is the point.

"The right introduction at the right moment is worth more than any conference, panel or industry event. We don't wait for those moments — we create them."

Paulette Mirzikinian — Founder, Circle8

How We Work

Three Ways to the Table

01 Introductions Access to the right people across every sector of the design and construction industry — architects, developers, builders, financiers, consultants, suppliers and buyers.
02 Acquisitions Assembling the right team around a site or opportunity — from pre-acquisition through to tender, securing the expertise needed to move a project from concept to commitment.
03 Live Projects Bringing the right people into projects already in motion — whether in design, procurement, delivery or pre-sales — through to post-completion and handover.
8 Maximum Seats Every table is capped at eight. Enough depth to solve real problems. Small enough that every person at the table matters.

In Practice

From First Call to the Right Room

Each engagement begins with understanding the opportunity — then building the right table around it.

Service 01

Introductions Across Sectors

If you need access to a sector you don't yet have a relationship in — a developer looking for the right architect, a builder needing a financier, a supplier seeking the right procurement contact — we make that introduction with context, not just a name.

Every introduction is considered. We only connect people where there is a genuine reason for both parties to be in the room.

For: Developers · Architects · Builders · Financiers · Consultants · Suppliers

Service 02

Acquisition & Tender Assembly

You have a site or an opportunity. You need the right team to take it forward — whether that's putting together a consortium for an acquisition, assembling the right consultants for a feasibility, or building a compelling tender response.

We identify who needs to be at the table, make the connections, and help structure the collaboration from pre-acquisition through to commitment.

For: Developers · Consultants · Builders · Financiers

Service 03

Live Project & Post-Completion

For projects already underway — in design development, procurement, construction or pre-sales — we bring in the right people where a gap exists. We also support the final stages: pre-completion sales strategy, agent introductions and post-handover relationships.

No project is too far along. The right introduction at the right stage changes what's possible.

For: Developers · Architects · Builders · Sales Agents

Why Circle8

We Know Who Needs to Be in the Room

Circle8 has spent years building relationships across every sector of the design and construction industry — from developers, architects and builders through to financiers, consultants, suppliers and sales agents. That network is what makes the Round Table work.

We don't match people from a database. We know the people at the table personally — their capabilities, their current priorities, and whether they are genuinely the right fit for the opportunity in front of them.

This is a service built on discretion, depth of relationship, and an honest understanding of how this industry actually works.

What You Can Expect

Considered Introductions

Every connection is made with purpose. You will know why you are being introduced and what the opportunity looks like for both parties.

Direct Access

No intermediaries, no mass introductions. When Paulette brings people to the table, she is present in the room.

Industry Breadth

Relationships that span the full project lifecycle — acquisition, design, finance, construction, delivery, sales and beyond.

Absolute Discretion

Live opportunities are sensitive. Everything discussed at the Round Table stays at the Round Table until both parties choose to move forward.

Bring Your Opportunity

Tell Us What You Need

If you have a live opportunity — a site, a project, a gap in your team, or a sector you need access to — we want to hear about it. Paulette will review every submission personally and respond with whether and how Circle8 can help.

There is no cost to submit. If we can help, we will outline what that looks like before any commitment is made.
